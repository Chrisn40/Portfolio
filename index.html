Social Media Campaign Stop Think Detect

A promotional Instagram post designed to raise awareness for the 'Stop Think Detect' deepfake game. Bold visuals, punchy copy, and a clear call-to-action guide users to download the app while maintaining brand consistency across digital platforms.

Ala al-Hawiyyeh - Arabic Book Design

War & Love" Illustrated Narrative

A visual narrative based on true events from Lebanons civil war, following two students trapped between militia violence and a desperate fight for survival. This project combines graphic storytelling with historical research to depict themes of resilience, memory, and the human cost of conflict.

Chocolate Packaging Design

Luxury Chocolate Packaging Gifts & Retail

A sleek, market-ready packaging system designed for premium chocolates sold in supermarkets and special events. This project involved creating eye-catching wrappers, structural packaging, and cohesive branding to elevate the unboxing experience while ensuring practicality for mass production.

Typography

Strange Tales Arabic Typography & Book Design

A typographic exploration of surreal neighborhood stories, crafted into a visually striking Arabic book. This project merges experimental type design with narrative tension, using InDesign to balance readability and artistic expression while honoring Arabic scripts fluidity.
